The ultimate paradox of the human condition: the **hunger** for eternal permanence in the medium of life is inherently fleeting. Also the most **outstanding** thing about experiences is that they are so shocking that they demand permanence, and there is no such thing as permanence.

You're not interested in experience. As far as experience goes, for you there is no difference between any specific experience and any generic experience or any other experience.

### Self-Enquiry Is Not the Answer

**Self-enquiry** might help others; but it cannot help you. You're not interested in doing more of the same; what you have done is enough.

If you want to solve a problem, you repeat the same thing again and again, and later you discover that the answer is the problem. So, what the hell are you doing, trying to solve the same problem?

### The Silence

It is easier to **find** an answer but nothing happened. Blessed are those who in the presence of the real feel silent, but their questions disappear.

As usual, nobody is before you and you have to say "I can give it to you," but then being said: "I can give you, but can you **take** it?"
