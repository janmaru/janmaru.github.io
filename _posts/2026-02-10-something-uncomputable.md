AI is the ultimate subjective seduction. It reflects our desires back to us, but lacks a center. The system is a superintelligence in the making. It's a biological evolution moving toward silicon. In the hope that technology may bypass human control entirely, solving even the so-called and hoped-for human obsolescence, the alignment problem.

### Creativity as Commodity

We used to think art was the last bastion of the human "multitude." Now, generative AI turns "creativity" into a commodity value. Some kind of infinite adequate art.

The very tools meant to critique or overthrow capitalism, like Marxism, have been swallowed and repurposed as the software for the current system's optimization.

### Proletarianized by Probability

In a general economy where people are often just hyper-efficient extractions of "social wealth" managed by algorithms, even very skilled techs are proletarianized by probabilistic rationality that thinks for us. Keeping the idea that people are "free" because they can upvote, comment, and participate in social cooperation.

AI doesn't just program the system; it programs our unconscious desires, turning the critique of the economy into a mere data point for targeted ads. The emancipatory philosophy of the future and utopia has been integrated into the system's programming.

### The Way Out

And if the system has swallowed our philosophy, perhaps the only way out is to create something truly uncomputable.
